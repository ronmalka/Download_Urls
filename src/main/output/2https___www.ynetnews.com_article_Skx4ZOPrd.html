Smotrich calls on Bennett to support Netanyahu for premiership

Moran Azulay |
Published: 04.04.21 , 20:07
Religious Zionist leader Bezalel Smotrich called on Yamina head Naftali Bennett Sunday, telling him that "any recommendation to the president that is not Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu - will bring us closer to a left-wing government."
"Naftali, do not be starstruck by the left's embrace of you, do not prevent tomorrow the formation of a right-wing government," he said.
